Audio Reactive Sensor

Flash your ESP32 device with the audio-reactive firmware for use with Aqara Advanced Lighting. Connect your device via USB and click Install.

M5Stack ATOM Echo

~$13, built-in PDM microphone, USB-C. Primary recommended device.

Note: After flashing, connect to the "audio-reactive" WiFi hotspot to configure your network credentials. The device will then appear in Home Assistant automatically.
